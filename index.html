📘 Student Insight App

A privacy-first, free, open-source analytics tool for any learning institution. Connect your Google Sheet — all analysis runs in your browser.

First time? Click New Session to set up your class and connect your Google Sheet.  ·  Adding Test 2 / Test 3? Resume your session below — your sheet already has the new data.  ·  Returning? Pick a session tile below to continue.
